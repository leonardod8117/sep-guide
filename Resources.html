Resources


gDoc/gDrawing

Using either one of these serivces provided by Google can be helpful with planning how a project will look and what content will be used. Both Google Docs and Google Drawing are easy to use and accessible provided that you have a Gmail. Simply planning your project is essential to organizing your ideas, which will serve as the basic guideline to your project.

w3schools

You will find w3schools very useful in developing your understanding of the subjects taught in SEP. With simple explanations followed by practices, w3schools allows you to learn quick and immediately apply what you learned.

Google

Simply typing your question into Google is a quick way to access multiple resources that can help you. In order to get the most helpful resources, type in specific questions.
