Opportunities

A wide variety of opportunities will be available to SEP students. Students will be doing everything from going on professional field trips to going to special events.


Field trips

Students will be attending field trips to perfessional business that offer opportunities. One field trip that previous students have gone on is to Deloitte.

Students will not only be limited to field trips such as deloitte. They will also be going to big time places like Google and Squarespace

Special Events

There will also be special events such as going to hackathons

More Opportunities

Along side event and field trips, there will alos be extra opportunities out side of school. You can get job internships in places like Pathfinders and more. (These will be paid jobs over the summer)
