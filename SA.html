Grading Categories

Focus

Classwork and participation will be a part of your report card grade. In order to get the credit, students must actively participate in class from classroom discussions to working collaboratively with a partner.

Completion

Homework is no doubt a significant part of the SEP class. Students will be given assignments and other things to take home and finish. Although very few homework will be given throughout the year, it is still important to complete them. Missing too much will negativity effect the average grade.

Hard Work

This is the largest and most significant part of the overall grade. Students will be expected to take a multitude of tests, quizzes and projects. The tests and quizzes will test what a student does and does not know while a project will have specific guidelines to follow. The projects and tests will be graded at the end and averaged into the overall grade.
